What is Financial Literacy?

Financial literacy means understanding how money works. It teaches you how to manage your finances, make smart decisions, and plan for the future. Knowing the basics of money management, such as budgeting, saving, and investing, will set you on the path to financial success.

Budgeting Skills

Budgeting is a tool that helps you manage your money. By knowing where your money is going, you can save more and avoid debt! A budget allows you to track your income and expenses, helping you make better decisions about where your money goes and how to save for future goals.

Introduction to Investments
